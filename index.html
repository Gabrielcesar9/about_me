About me

I'm Gabriel

I am a vibrant and experienced English educator with 7 years of dedicated service to teaching the English language. Throughout my career, I have been working to provide engaging and effective English instruction to students of different backgrounds and age groups. I have international experience, having lived in the United States for a year and a half, which has contributed to my development as a teacher and enriched my teaching approach. At 28 years old, I am passionate about creating inclusive and inspiring learning environments, empowering students to achieve confidence and fluency in the English language. I am excited to share my enthusiasm and knowledge with you on this learning journey.
